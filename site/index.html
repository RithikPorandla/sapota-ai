A three-layer pipeline, built and validated

Every US coastal state has latent asset data and public satellite imagery. We turn that into a ranked, budgeted, defensible repair queue.

Layer 1 · Detection

U-Net + ResNet34 segmentation trained on Sentinel-2 imagery with Massachusetts CZM structures as ground truth. 9,543 structures indexed.

Layer 2 · Temporal change

Seven years of multi-index composite scoring (NDVI, NDWI, brightness). Type-aware weighting, per-structure normalization, CZM-to-CZM matched-pair validation.

Layer 3 · Risk engine

Fuses NAIP 60cm aerial with FEMA flood zones, property value at risk, storm history, and USACE unit-rate repair cost estimates.
