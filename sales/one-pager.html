What the town gets

  • Every coastal protection structure in town, scored 0–100 for repair priority
  • Interactive dashboard: map, priority queue, per-structure detail, NDVI time series
  • Printable report suitable for Select Board or Town Meeting
  • Per-structure estimated repair cost (USACE unit rates) and value at risk
  • Storm-triggered reassessment within 72 hours of a named storm

How it's built

  • L1 detection — U-Net + ResNet34 on Sentinel-2, trained on CZM ground truth
  • L2 temporal — 7-year change (2018–2024), CZM 2006 vs 2015 matched-pair validated
  • L3 risk — NAIP 60cm fused with FEMA flood zones, property value, storm history
  • All data sources free or public. No drones, no field crews, no hardware.
