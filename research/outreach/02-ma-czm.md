## Why this email works (don't change it)

- **Credentials up front without bragging.** UMass Dartmouth + Vineyard Wind positions you as "local serious person," not a random founder.
- **Cites their data first, not your product.** The email is about CZM's work extending — not a product pitch. That's the correct posture for a first email to a state agency.
- **One specific number (0.443).** Technical people open subject lines with numbers.
- **Three concrete things to discuss, ranked.** Ask 1 is free (they'll enjoy the technical walk-through). Ask 2 is institutional (advances their mission). Ask 3 is the actual goal. Pyramid structure.
- **Closes with a specific date and a physical address.** Signals seriousness and that you know where they are.
